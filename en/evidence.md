# evidence (Supplementary Note)

> This is a brief English-language guide to the `evidence/` directory, not a full translation. The Japanese files (`evidence/README.md`, `evidence/wikimedia.md`, `evidence/facebook.md`, `evidence/release.md`) are authoritative. Screenshots and external links are not translated, since most of the underlying source pages (Wikimedia Commons discussions, in particular) are already in English.

## What this directory is for

`evidence/` collects supporting material relevant to prior use and public recognition of the Gikoneko/AAAP-related works, as referenced in AAAPS-v1.md Article 3. It combines the external verifiability of GitHub's commit history with a record of activity on the project's Facebook community.

These materials are supporting/contextual records, not a legal determination in themselves. Evaluation of their legal significance is left to experts and third parties.

## Contents

- **wikimedia.md** — A fact-only timeline of public records on Wikimedia Commons and Japanese Wikipedia relating to "AAAP" and "Gikoneko," including a 2006 article deletion discussion, a 2013 category deletion discussion, and the category's deletion in 2015. No evaluative claims are made; the timeline simply documents what happened and when.
- **facebook.md** — A record confirming that the project's Facebook page is publicly viewable while logged out (private browsing), dated 2026-07-07.
- **release.md** — A record of the v1.0 public release itself: the GitHub push, an Internet Archive (Wayback Machine) snapshot, and announcements on X and Facebook, all dated 2026-07-07.

## Operating principle

Materials are preserved in their original form (screenshots, header information, etc.) wherever possible and are not altered, in keeping with this project's "do not tamper with the archive" principle. This applies equally to unfavorable records (e.g. category deletions) as to favorable ones.
